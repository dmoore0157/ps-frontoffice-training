About PreventScripts

PreventScripts Training · Module 1 of 8

What is PreventScripts?

PreventScripts is a lifestyle medicine and remote patient monitoring (RPM) platform that partners with primary care practices to help patients prevent and manage chronic conditions like obesity, diabetes, and hypertension — all through their mobile phone.

If the video above doesn't load, try refreshing the page or contact support.

How It Works

When a provider identifies a patient at risk for metabolic conditions, they enroll the patient into the PreventScripts program. Here's what happens next:

1. Assessment: Patients complete a health risk assessment in the waiting room before their visit, giving providers real-time data.

2. Enrollment: The provider enrolls eligible patients and they download the PreventScripts app.

3. Starter Kit: Patients receive a healthy living kit at home — including a Bluetooth-connected scale, water bottle, healthy plate magnet, and program instructions.

4. Daily Tracking: Patients track health habits like water intake, nutrition, and steps in the app.

5. My Plans™ Goals: Patients set simple, evidence-based goals (Drink More Water, Eat More Vegetables, Move More, etc.) and receive daily coaching tips.

6. Monthly Sessions: A Health Advocacy Coach (HAC) from the care team meets with the patient monthly to review progress, address barriers, and set next steps.

Why This Matters for You

As front desk staff, you're often the first point of contact when patients have questions about the program. You don't need to be a clinical expert — you just need to know enough to confidently explain what it is, guide patients to schedule through the app, and connect them with the right support when needed. That's what the rest of this training covers.

⚠ Critical Rule — Do NOT Schedule for Patients

Front desk and reception staff should never schedule PreventScripts sessions on behalf of patients. This has caused significant issues at other clinics. Patients must schedule through the app themselves. Your role is to guide them, not do it for them.

Common Scenarios & Scripts

PreventScripts Training · Module 4 of 8

What to Say When Patients Ask

Click each scenario to see the recommended response.

📅

"Can you schedule my appointment for me?"

▾
"I need to schedule my next session. Can you just do it for me?"
Your Response
"I'd be happy to help! PreventScripts sessions are scheduled through your app — it's quick and easy. Open the app, tap Sessions at the bottom, then tap the orange Schedule a Session button. Or tap the Preventy assistant (the P icon) and say 'Schedule a new session.' Want to try it now while I'm here?"
📱

"I don't have the app" or "I don't have a smartphone"

▾
"I don't have the app on my phone" / "I don't use a smartphone"
If they have a smartphone but no app
"No problem! Download PreventScripts from the App Store or Google Play — just search 'PreventScripts.' It's free. Use the enrollment link your care team provided to set up your account."
If they don't have a smartphone
"I understand. The PreventScripts support team can help figure out the best option for you: (859) 287-4977 or support@preventscripts.com."
❓

"What is PreventScripts?" / "What's this new program?"

▾
"Someone mentioned a new health program. What is it?"
Your Response
"PreventScripts is a free wellness program offered through our practice. It's an app that helps you set health goals — like improving blood pressure, managing weight, or being more active — and connects you with a Health Advocacy Coach who meets with you monthly. Your doctor enrolled you because they believe it will benefit your health."
🔑

"I forgot my password" / "I can't log in"

▾
"I can't get into the app."
Your Response
"Try the 'Forgot Password' option on the login screen first. If that doesn't work, call PreventScripts support at (859) 287-4977 or email support@preventscripts.com — they're available M–F, 9 AM to 5 PM Eastern."
🔄

"I need to reschedule or cancel"

▾
"Something came up. Can you change my appointment?"
Your Response
"You can reschedule or cancel right from the app. Tap Sessions, then Schedule a Session. The Preventy assistant gives you options to reschedule or cancel. You can also tap the P icon and say 'Reschedule my session.'"
💰

"Is this going to cost me anything?"

▾
"How much does this cost?"
Your Response
"PreventScripts is included as part of your care with our practice. Most patients have no out-of-pocket cost for the program, but that can depend on your specific insurance plan or employer benefits. If you'd like to know exactly what your plan covers, our billing team can look into that for you."
😤

Patient insists you schedule for them

▾
"I don't want to use the app. Can't you just do it?"
Stay Friendly but Firm
"I completely understand. Unfortunately, I'm not able to schedule PreventScripts sessions from our system — it's only done through the app. But it's really easy! If you have your phone, I can walk you through it right now — takes less than a minute. Or the support team can help at (859) 287-4977."

Remember: Never schedule on behalf of the patient, even under pressure.

Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

PreventScripts Training · Module 5 of 8

Keep This at Your Workstation

Print this page and pin it where you can see it every day.

✅ DO

  • Guide patients to use the app themselves
  • Walk patients through scheduling on their phone
  • Provide the support phone number and email
  • Explain PreventScripts as a free wellness program
  • Reassure patients the app is simple to use
  • Refer complex questions to PreventScripts support

🚫 DON'T

  • Schedule sessions on behalf of patients
  • Access the patient's app or account for them
  • Promise specific billing details you're unsure of
  • Make clinical recommendations about the program
  • Give login credentials or reset passwords yourself
  • Ignore frustration — empathize, then redirect

📋 Scheduling Steps (App)

  • Open the PreventScripts app
  • Tap Sessions in the bottom nav
  • Tap + Schedule a session
  • Tap Schedule a new session
  • Select a time or type a preference
  • Confirm — done!

🤖 Alternative: Preventy AI

  • Tap the circled P icon on any screen
  • Select Schedule a new session
  • Choose a time slot or type a request
  • Also handles reschedule & cancel

  • In-app chat: M–F, 9 AM – 5 PM EST
